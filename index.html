PlumbTally

Account Deletion

Overview

You can permanently delete your PlumbTally account and all associated data — either directly in the app or by contacting support.

Delete in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the PlumbTally app:

  1. Open the app and go to your Profile.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm the first prompt.
  4. Type DELETE in the confirmation field and tap Delete permanently.

Your account and all data are deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

Request via email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to:

support@plumbtally.app

Include the email address associated with your account. Requests are processed within 7 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account (email, identity)
  • All projects
  • All items and line items
  • All payments
  • Your business profile
  • Subscription and trial data
  • Local app data on your device

What is not stored

  • PDF files — generated on-device and never uploaded to our servers
  • Payment card details — no payment provider is integrated

Processing time

In-app deletion: immediate.
Email requests: processed within 7 days.
